# 人像摄影提示词模板

## 人像七层模型

```
[相机/媒介] → [光线环境] → [人物五官] → [服装造型] → [姿势动作] → [表情眼神] → [画质排除词]
```

## 五种经典人像光线方案

### 1. 霓虹混合光（都市夜景人像）
```
Harsh convenience store fluorescent lighting from inside mixed with
colorful pink and blue neon glow from outside signs, realistic reflections
on glass door, cold-warm color temperature contrast on skin
```
效果：冷白荧光灯+彩色霓虹灯对比，都市感十足。@BubbleBrain 代表作。

### 2. 柔光窗光（日系/清新人像）
```
Soft diffused natural window light, slight overexposure on highlights,
pastel tones, low contrast, gentle light wrapping around subject,
airy and bright atmosphere
```

### 3. 机顶直闪（Y2K/街头快照风）
```
Harsh direct on-camera flash, specular highlights on skin and clothing,
deep shadows behind subject, strong catchlights in eyes, high contrast
flash aesthetic, authentic compact camera feel
```

### 4. 暖色环境光（和风/温泉/居家）
```
Soft ambient wooden lantern lighting mixed with gentle natural window light,
warm orange glow, rim lighting highlighting skin and fabric texture,
cozy intimate atmosphere
```

### 5. Rembrandt 光（经典肖像/杂志大片）
```
Rembrandt lighting setup, single key light from 45 degrees,
triangle of light on shadow-side cheek, dark dramatic background,
chiaroscuro effect, classic portrait painterly quality
```

## 四种热门人像风格模板

### 韩系偶像写真风
```
K-pop idol photoshoot style, Canon EOS R5 photography. Early 20s Korean
female idol with flawless glass skin, sparkling doe eyes with natural
double eyelids, small delicate nose, heart-shaped lips, straight
jet-black hair with curtain bangs, wearing oversized cream-colored
knit sweater. Sitting cross-legged on a white studio floor, looking
directly at camera with soft gentle smile. Studio lighting with softbox
and beauty dish, pastel lavender background, high key, clean and polished
commercial look. No plastic skin, no over-airbrushing, no watermark.
```

### 日系胶片复古风
```
Fujifilm Superia 400 color film photography, natural grain, muted greens
and warm skin tones, slight cyan shift in shadows. A 22-year-old Japanese
woman with bob-cut hair, wearing a vintage floral one-piece dress, walking
through a narrow Tokyo alley in afternoon sunlight. Dappled light through
overhead laundry lines, warm golden hour backlight creating hair glow.
Candid moment, natural relaxed expression, looking slightly away from
camera. Shallow depth of field, analog warmth, documentary style.
No digital sharpening, no watermark.
```

### 电影质感风
```
Cinematic portrait, anamorphic lens, widescreen 2.39:1 composition.
A woman in her late 20s with sharp angular features, wet hair slicked back,
wearing a black leather jacket, standing in a rain-soaked neon alley at night.
Dramatic chiaroscuro lighting — harsh blue neon from the left, warm amber
street lamp from the right. Atmospheric haze, rain particles catching the light,
wet reflections on pavement. Cinematic color grading in teal and orange,
film grain overlay, 35mm cinema film stock. No blur, no watermark.
```

### 古风国风
```
Traditional Chinese ink wash painting aesthetic meets modern fashion portrait.
A young woman wearing white hanfu with red embroidery, standing in a misty
bamboo forest at dawn. Ink wash texture bleeding into the image edges,
calligraphy brush strokes in the background. Long black hair flowing in
the wind, ethereal expression, gaze slightly downward. Vermillion and
ink black dominant color palette with jade green accents. Negative space
composition occupying 40% of the image. Cinematic lighting with traditional
Chinese color grading. No modern elements, no watermark.
```
